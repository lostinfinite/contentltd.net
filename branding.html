Branding Guidelines

These guidelines outline the proper use of ContentLTD's brand assets, including our logo, colors, and typography. Consistent branding helps us maintain a strong, recognizable identity across all platforms.

1. Logo Usage

  • Use the official ContentLTD logo provided in contentltd_white.webp (for dark backgrounds) and contentltd_black.webp (for light backgrounds).
  • Do not alter, stretch, or recolor the logo.
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least half the logo's height.
  • Do not use the logo on backgrounds that reduce its visibility or contrast.

2. Brand Colors

  • Primary Accent: #FFBB00
  • Background Dark: #1d1d1d
  • Background Accent: #000000
  • Text: #f5f5f5

3. Typography

  • Headings: Inter, sans-serif
  • Body: Open Sans, sans-serif

4. Do's and Don'ts

  • Do use the logo and colors as provided.
  • Do not modify or distort the logo.
  • Do not use unapproved colors or fonts.
  • Do not use the logo in a way that implies partnership or endorsement without permission.

5. Questions

If you have questions about using ContentLTD's brand assets, please contact us on Discord.
