ContentLTD Community Standards

Last Updated: 7/16/25

Effective: 7/16/25

Established: 6/25/25

At ContentLTD℠, user safety is a priority. These Community Standards exist to protect everyone who uses our services, whether you're roleplaying, moderating, chatting, or building something new. They define what is acceptable behavior across all ContentLTD platforms, including our Discord servers, ER:LC game environments, websites, and tools.

These rules are not just about punishment. They are about setting clear expectations so that users, creators, and server owners can interact without fear of abuse, harassment, or exploitation. We want our spaces to be structured, respectful, and free from harmful conduct.

Everyone is responsible for following these Standards. Staff and server owners are expected to enforce them. Users are expected to report serious issues and avoid behavior that creates risk for others.

These Community Standards support the Terms of Service and help keep our platform safe, functional, and fair. If you’re unsure whether something is allowed, ask first. These rules are here to protect you and everyone else.

Safety

We want everyone who engages with the communities under our umbrella, where everyone can feel comfortable and is guaranteed safety. To ensure ContentLTD is a place where everyone feels comfortable being themselves, the following is not allowed:

Child Exploitation

ContentLTD has a zero tolerance policy for the exploitation of minors or anyone. This includes:

  • Any predatory behavior, including attempting to befriend a minor to manipulate and exploit them (i.e., grooming)
  • Sexualizing minors in any way
  • Engaging in sexual conversation with or soliciting sexual material from minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or discussing child sexual exploitation material
  • Sexual extortion of children
  • Child sex trafficking
  • Coercion and enticement of a minor to engage in illegal sexual activity

Terrorism

We have a zero tolerance policy for anything that incites, condones, supports, glorifies, or promotes any terrorist or extremist organization or individual. And their ideology or actions include:

  • Depictions of or support for terrorist or extremist attacks
  • Depictions of or support for the leaders or representatives of terrorist or extremist organizations
  • Sharing the slogans, images, flags, manifestos, or icons of terrorist or extremist organizations, either in whole or in readily identifiable part
  • References to the ideologies, messages, or strategies of terrorist and extremist organizations
  • Expressing support, condoning, or glorifying terrorist extremist ideologies or actions
  • Recruiting membership for a terrorist or extremist organization, or encouraging others to leave ContentLTD to find such information
  • Fundraising for terrorist or extremist organizations, people, or groups
  • Expressing support, condoning, or glorifying mass shootings and other acts of domestic terrorism or violent extremism

Threats, Bullying, Harassment

We do not support the threatening, bullying, or harassment of others. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Threatening physical or sexual assault or violence
  • Threatening to harm someone in real life
  • Revealing or threatening to reveal others’ personal information
  • Threatening to take over another’s Roblox account or to file false abuse reports against another user
  • Singling out a user or group for ridicule or abuse, either publicly or privately
  • Sexual harassment
  • Impersonating individuals, groups, or entities in ways that may damage their reputation or cause others to harm them, either online or in real life
  • Threatening any sort of civil or legal action against the ContentLTD Brand, individual staff members of the brand, or any third parties affiliated with the brand

Suicide, Self-Injury, and Other Harmful Behavior

We take our users’ well-being very seriously. We’re here to help provide resources for those struggling with mental health, and we reserve the right to communicate with authorities regarding credible threats of self-harm. We do not allow content or behavior that depicts, glorifies, or encourages suicide, self-injury, or real-world activities that may create an extreme risk of physical harm, including:

Describing methods for suicide or self-harm

Content that glorifies, supports, or depicts instances or methods of suicide or self-harm

Dangerous physical challenges or stunts in the real world

If you or a loved one is having thoughts of suicide or self-harm, there is help available. Reach out to a suicide prevention hotline like the one found here. For additional global resources, here is a good place to start.

Discrimination, Slurs, and Hate Speech

We honor and welcome users of all ages, backgrounds, and identities. We do not allow content or behavior that supports, glorifies, or promotes hate groups, their ideologies, or actions. You also may not discriminate, mock, or promote hatred against individuals or groups, or encourage others to do so directly or indirectly, on the basis of their:

  • Age
  • Race, perceived race, or ethnicity
  • National origin
  • Sexual orientation
  • Gender, gender identity, or gender expression
  • Religion or religious affiliation, or beliefs
  • Disability status, including diseases, bodily conditions, disfigurement, mobility issues, and mental impairment
  • Veteran status
  • Caste
  • Familial status

Harmful Off-Platform Behavior

We believe that everyone should treat everyone with respect, both on and off of Roblox. If credibly reported to us, we may take action against users who, among other things:

  • Are associated with organized crime, terrorist or extremist organizations, or hate groups
  • Harass, bully, discriminate, or harm others outside of Roblox
  • Share others’ personal information off-platform, including making false reports to authorities
  • Have been convicted of certain egregious crimes, engage in certain illegal activities, or encourage others to do so

Civility

To protect our users and uphold a fair and respectful environment, ContentLTD prohibits the following categories of content across all services, including affiliated servers, bots, and user-generated material:

Real-World Tragedies and Sensitive Events

Content that references or exploits real-world tragedies is not allowed. This includes content that depicts, mimics, celebrates, or profits from events such as:

  • Acts of mass violence or terrorism
  • Natural disasters or large-scale accidents
  • Major civil or human rights violations
  • High-profile criminal cases or sensational crimes
  • Political conflicts involving borders or sovereignty
  • We do not permit content that mocks victims, glorifies perpetrators, or attempts to use these events for shock value or commercial gain.

Violence, Gore, and Abuse

Graphic violence, gore, and depictions of serious physical or psychological harm are strictly prohibited. This includes:

  • Animal cruelty or torture
  • Realistic portrayals of death or extreme injury
  • Torture or abuse of people, even in fictionalized or “edgy” contexts
  • Shock content or materials intended to disturb or disgust
  • Any violence depicted must remain within clearly fictional, stylized, or game-related contexts. Explicit realism or cruelty is not allowed.

Sexual and Romantic Content

ContentLTD does not allow any form of sexual material or romantic solicitation. This includes:

  • Attempts to form real-world romantic relationships on our platform
  • Flirtatious or suggestive communication between users
  • Any discussion or display of sexual activity, imagery, or nudity
  • Avatars, bundles, or assets that appear sexually provocative
  • Conversations, jokes, or media involving explicit sexual content
  • Modesty, professionalism, and age-appropriate interactions are required at all times. Violations will be moderated without exception.

Drugs, Alcohol, and Illegal Behavior

Users may not promote, discuss, or depict illegal or regulated substances or criminal activity. This includes:

  • Any reference to real-world recreational drugs, alcohol, or tobacco
  • Depictions of intoxication or abuse of substances
  • Mentions of real-world firearms, explosives, or homemade weapons
  • Tutorials or discussions related to manufacturing weapons
  • Sales or promotion of any restricted goods or paraphernalia
  • Additionally, no content should encourage breaking the law or imply that doing so is acceptable or entertaining.

Gambling and Contests

No gambling-related content may be offered or simulated through our platform. This applies to:

  • Games of chance offering Robux, currency, or any item of value
  • Sweepstakes or lotteries of any kind
  • Casino-style simulations with betting, even if fictional
  • Real-money gambling references, promotions, or links
  • Imagery or set dressing that loosely references gambling (e.g., slot machine art) may be permitted in a clearly fictional, unplayable setting, but must not involve user interaction.

Profanity and Inappropriate Language

All services under ContentLTD must remain free of offensive language used in offensive context(s). This includes:

  • Any use of profanity, even when altered with characters or symbols used to offend any third party, or user.
  • Use of music, quotes, or sound effects containing slurs or swearing in a way to offend users or third parties.
  • Slurs, hate speech, or other toxic language in any form

Political Content and Campaigning

To maintain neutrality and avoid division, ContentLTD prohibits political content involving real-world figures or organizations. This includes:

  • Mention or depiction of political officials or candidates
  • Political slogans, campaign material, or election references
  • Political party promotion or fundraising of any kind
  • Desecration of flags, national symbols, or other inflammatory content
  • We do allow historical education or civic discussion where appropriate, but it must not promote, endorse, or defame real political movements or figures.

These rules are not negotiable. Violations will result in action, which may include content removal, warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans. ContentLTD is committed to a space that prioritizes user safety, professionalism, and shared responsibility. If you have questions or concerns about this policy, contact moderation support directly.

Integrity

ContentLTD prohibits all forms of dishonest behavior that give users an unfair advantage, mislead others, or undermine platform integrity. Users and creators are expected to follow fair practices when interacting with our systems, tools, and community.

Cheating, Scams, and Account Exploitation

Any attempt to cheat, exploit, or manipulate services for personal gain or to harm others is strictly forbidden. This includes:

  • Using or distributing exploits to gain unfair advantages in any experience or system
  • Encouraging or instructing others to cheat
  • Misleading users with false product descriptions or deceptive trades
  • Impersonating another person or misrepresenting identity for any purpose
  • Attempting to access someone else’s account, credentials, or private data
  • Posting or linking to phishing websites or scam pages targeting account credentials
  • Our moderation systems and team will take action against any behavior intended to deceive, exploit, or abuse the trust of other users.

Spam and Platform Disruption

We do not allow spam or behavior that disrupts services or misleads users, including:

  • Uploading or posting repetitive or misleading content to gain visibility
  • Using exaggerated or false metadata to manipulate search or engagement
  • Creating experiences designed to send users off-platform
  • Sending unsolicited messages, invites, or promotions to users
  • Any form of spamming or artificial engagement will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account restrictions.

Intellectual Property and Copyright Infringement

Respecting others' work is required. Do not use copyrighted or trademarked content without permission. Examples of violations include:

  • Reusing another developer’s code or systems without consent
  • Uploading images, audio, or art created by someone else without a license
  • Using logos, branding, or trade dress from companies, games, or creators you do not represent

If a valid DMCA or trademark complaint is filed, the content will be removed. Repeat violations may lead to asset deletion or suspension of access to our services.

Security

A safe community depends not only on respectful behavior but also on protecting each other's digital privacy. Users and creators must treat personal data responsibly and take steps to secure their own accounts. Protecting sensitive information and following safety practices is essential to keeping our platforms safe for everyone.

Protecting Personal and Private Information

Users may not share, request, or collect personal data from others while using ContentLTD platforms. This includes:

  • Email addresses
  • Passwords or login tokens
  • Residential addresses or real-time locations
  • Bank details, credit card numbers, or financial records
  • Health or medical information
  • Phone numbers
  • Social security numbers or other sensitive credentials
  • Non-Roblox usernames, social handles, or screen names
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings of real individuals who are not public figures
  • Full names, unless specifically allowed in limited profile settings for eligible users
  • We do not tolerate attempts to bypass filters, disguise personal information, or trick others into disclosing private data.

Account and Platform Security

All users are responsible for keeping their accounts secure. We strongly recommend enabling available security features, including two-factor authentication, and never sharing login credentials. Accounts found to be compromising security, either intentionally or through neglect, may face restrictions.

For your own protection, never engage in account trading, password sharing, or the use of unauthorized third-party tools. These practices risk your data and the security of the platform as a whole.

Redirecting Users Off-Platform

You may not use ContentLTD services to send users to external websites or services except in limited approved cases. The only external links permitted on ContentLTD-managed platforms must relate to verified services and must follow these rules:

  • Links to approved platforms such as YouTube, Discord, Twitch, X (Twitter), Facebook, and Guilded are only allowed when embedded properly using supported systems such as social profile settings or dedicated platform APIs
  • No links, QR codes, shortened URLs, or hidden redirects may be posted in chat, media, or descriptions unless explicitly authorized
  • Bypassing these rules by splitting or altering URLs, using misleading redirects, or embedding unapproved links will be treated as a violation

Servers affiliated with ContentLTD may post external links, but only server staff are permitted to do so. General users are not allowed to share links, regardless of the platform or context, unless explicitly authorized.
