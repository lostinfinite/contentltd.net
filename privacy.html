  1.  Introduction

Welcome to the ContentLTD Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we (“we”, “us”, “ContentLTD”) will use users (“users”, “you”, or “your”) information. We do not knowingly collect data from users that are under the age of 13, if you are under the age of 13 then we ask that you don't use our services in accordance with our Terms of Service and Community Standards.

Before moving on, we use certain phrases and we need to define them.

“Minor” means any individual between the ages of 13 - 17.

“Adult”, “Parent”, or “Guardian” is the parent or caretaker of any Minor. Someone who has sole or majority custody.

“Identifying Documents” is any document used to identify you as an individual; this can range from anything like a username to an ID, driver's License, or Birth Certificate.

“ID” is an Identification or driver's License that includes a Date of Birth and Full Legal Name.

“Private Server” is any server owned, operated, or maintained by ContentLTD.

“Platform” Means any various features and services, including websites, bots, applications, affiliated game servers, moderation tools, content, and custom functionalities  offered by ContentLTD

“Privacy Policy” is this policy.

  1.  Personally Identifiable Information

We do not knowingly collect Personally Identifiable Information (“PII”) outside of our official means (e.g, Appeals); however, we do our best to protect it. We will never sell, share, or otherwise make your PII known to any individual, third party, or organization. The only exception to this is if there is a request from Law Enforcement.

        For information on how we protect your data, please review Section 6.

  1.  Proving Your Age

If we believe you are under the age of 13, we may suspend your access to our services. You can appeal this decision at any time using our support form: https://contentltd.net/support. When you appeal an age-related suspension, we may request a clear photo of your face to help verify your age. If we are unable to confirm that you meet the minimum age requirement, we may ask for additional verification, which can include:

  • A valid photo ID or driver’s license
  • A one-time, non-refundable $1 USD payment made via credit or debit card through our payment processor, Stripe

Any photos or identification you submit will be reviewed only for the purpose of age verification and will be deleted promptly after the review is complete. We do not retain this data, and it is never shared outside of ContentLTD.

If you cannot verify your age through any of the methods above, your suspension will remain in place.

  1.  General Information We Collect

In general, we only collect little information on you, such as Emails, Discord and Roblox usernames, and/or profile link(s), Messages you send across our services, Cookies, IP Addresses, and Browser Information

  1.  How We Use Your Data

We collect your email to give you important updates regarding your access to ContentLTD Services.

We collect your Roblox and Discord usernames and/or profile link(s) if applicable to be able to identify who you are. There is no other purpose to this.

We collect certain messages you send across our services to screen them and ensure they are not consistent with raiding activities or raids. We do not store these messages long term, and they are deleted after screened. Note, it is impossible to request this data as we delete the messages as soon as we no longer need them.

We collect your IP Address to ensure that you are not in a country that we don't allow to use our services and to ensure that you are not using a VPN or Proxy.  Note, it is impossible to request this data as we delete the data as soon as we no longer need it.

Finally, we only collect Cookie and Browser information to help enforce bans across our services.

  1.  How We Secure Your Data

We take general precautions to protect the information users send to us. This applies whether the data comes through our website, support forms, or any other part of our service. The way we handle this information may vary depending on the context, but in many cases, some level of protection such as encryption or restricted access may be used.

Certain types of data, such as face photos, ID images, or written messages, are treated with additional care when necessary. We only use that kind of data for its intended purpose, such as verifying account ownership or age. Once we’ve completed the process that required the data, we typically remove it from our systems.

We do not intentionally share sensitive user information with others. That includes photos, private messages, or personal documentation submitted for support purposes. If there is a rare case where we are required to release information, such as by law, we’ll comply as needed, but these situations are uncommon.

The level of protection we apply may vary depending on how and where data is submitted. Not all parts of our platform are built with the same level of security, and users should keep that in mind when sharing any personal information. While we aim to prevent unauthorized access, we don’t guarantee full protection in every scenario.

No system is perfect. We can’t claim to be fully secure, and we don’t suggest that any platform can be. While we do what we can with the tools available to us, users should always use good judgment and only share what they are comfortable providing.
